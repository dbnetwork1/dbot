Thank you for choosing Watchdog

There are many large bots out there for the same purpose as Watchdog. But we hope
our accessability features and reliability will be enough to grow a small user base.

This page is designed for anyone new who is using Watchdog. Some of the settings
might be confusing, or you dont know what to set them to. This page will explain everything.

Ratelimit

The ratelimit is the required amount of offences to trigger the system

Without revealing the secrets behind our systems most of our features are measured overtime. The measured
elements are reset at a static ratemeaning that only we can change it. This duration is between 5 - 10 seconds.

Incase you were wondering what the 'Type' numbers in Weblogs refer too. Heres an index.

1: Guild Join
2: Guild Leave
3: Prefix Change
4: Mute & Unmute
5: Warning Added / Removed
6: All Guild warnings erased
7: Kick
8: Ban
9: Auto Moderator

~ Means approximately.
